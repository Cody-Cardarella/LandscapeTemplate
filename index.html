Who We Are

Northern Colorado's Outdoor Building Experts

StoneCraft Outdoors is a locally owned hardscaping and outdoor living company based in Fort Collins, Colorado. Since 2006, we've turned bare backyards into fully functional outdoor rooms — spaces where families gather, relax, and make memories.

We handle every project ourselves. No subcontracting, no cutting corners. Our crew is experienced, professional, and treats your property like their own.
